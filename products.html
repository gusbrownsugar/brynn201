The Infininte Created Imagination brought to Life

In this work of art Brynn manages to balance the infinite with the intimate. Upon lookin at this presentation we are shown the World and Universe being cared for by a woman. It's important that we remember there is always a higher power taking care of us.

The Lonely Tree

In this picturesque scene, Brynn is able to portray a strong tree with cool mild colors. This watercolor work of art shows us that in the midst of lonely times we can stay strong.

Natures Art The Garden

This piece of botanical art was created during Brynns time in Switzerland. On a visit to the Latter-Day Saint Temple, she picked the flowers and did what she does best. Now this botanical illustration serves as a reminder and a sort of "memory" in a jar.

The Cherry on Top Jewlery with Brynn

Wearable art, also known as Artwear or "art to wear", refers to art pieces in the shape of clothing or jewellery pieces. These pieces are usually handmade, and are produced only once or as a very limited series. With Brynns twist on nature and art you can be sure you will receive a piece of art unique to you.
