Creative Art, Creative Girl About Me

I was born in 2003 on a warm August day in St. George, Utah. My art career started when I was very young! I would make anything from paintings to jewlery and give it to the people I love the most! That's you! After winning awards and having various pieces of art displayed in the Springville Art Museum, I am ready to show my ART to the world! With your vision and my creativity, I am certain we can make your wildest dreams come true! So... buckle your seatbelts and get ready for the ride. My art will give you access to the farthest away lands and access to worlds you can only read about!
